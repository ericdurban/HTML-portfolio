The Best Movies According to Eric

My top 3 movies of all-time

Spirited Away

This is my favorite anime movie. The music always brings me back to the feelings when I first watched it. The story is well written and immerses you into a beautiful, creative world.

Inception

I love movies made by Christopher Nolan. This is the peak of what I feel his movies strive to achieve; cinematic wonder and character-driven narrative.

Everything, Everywhere, All At Once

This is one of my new favorite movies. I love it for how original, wacky, and over the top it is.
